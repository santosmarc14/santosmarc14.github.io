AG Fragrance is a website where customers can browse, select, and purchase perfumes over the internet. It typically features a catalog of items with descriptions, prices, and images, along with a shopping cart for managing selected products. Customers can place orders by providing their payment and shipping details through a secure checkout process. Online shops often include features like user reviews, search functionality, and personalized recommendations. They offer convenience by allowing shoppers to buy from anywhere at any time, and they enable businesses to reach a wider audience and operate with lower overhead costs.
