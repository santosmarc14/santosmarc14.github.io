Daily Time Record System

  • Category: Desktop Application
  • Client: School Project
  • Project date: 12 May, 2021

A Daily Time Record (DTR) system is a tool used by organizations to track and manage the attendance and working hours of employees. It allows employees to record their daily arrival and departure times, breaks, and any leave taken. The system calculates total work hours and overtime, and may include features for requesting and approving leave or schedule changes. DTR systems help ensure accuracy in payroll processing by providing reliable data on employee attendance. They also assist in monitoring compliance with labor laws and company policies regarding work hours and attendance. Overall, a DTR system improves efficiency in workforce management and helps organizations maintain transparency and accountability in tracking employee time.
