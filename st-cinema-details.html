ST CINEMA

ST Cinema

  • Category: Web Application
  • Client: School Project
  • Project date: 13 May, 2022

This Cinema Management Information System (CMIS) is a software tool designed to streamline and enhance the operations of a cinema. It helps manage various aspects of the cinema's activities, including scheduling movie showtimes, ticket sales, and seat reservations. The system tracks and manages inventory for concessions, oversees staff scheduling, and provides detailed reports on sales and attendance. It also offers features like online ticket booking, customer relationship management, and promotional campaign management. By automating these processes, a CMIS improves efficiency, enhances the customer experience, and provides valuable insights for better decision-making and operational management.
